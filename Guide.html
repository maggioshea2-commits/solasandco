Your step by step beauty routine

Step 1

Cleanse

Skin prep is arguably the most important step to ensure a smooth, even foundation for application. Use a gentle cleanser to wash away impurities.

Step 2

Moisturise

Apply a moisturiser containing SPF for protection against UV rays, exposure to which can gradually cause imperfections, wrinkles and health risks.

Step 3

Prime

Primer enhances the durability of makeup and an be chosen on the bases of skin texture, to control either dryness or oiliness.

Step 4

Foundation

Once shade matched to your unique complexion, apply foundation evenly and blend with a brush or sponge for a natural finish.

Step 5

Contour

Redefine the structure of your face by adding dimension with contour, enhancing your authentic beauty.

Step 6

Blush

Achieve the true Irish rosy cheeks by incorporating a pink coloured blush into your routine.

Step 7

Set in place

Seal the deal with a waterproof setting spray to ensure long lasting results, no matter the weather.
